First time we met

6 February 2021

My mom is probably still trying to wrap her head around the fact that I, the self-proclaimed shy guy, stumbled into a life-altering encounter. It all started with a random message on a day that would forever change the course of my life – thank you, universe, for conspiring in my favor. What began as a seemingly innocuous message evolved into endless chats, where Pooja and I discussed everything from the profound to the downright absurd (because, you know, studies and random sh*t go hand in hand). Thanks to a brief respite from the clutches of COVID, I made it to campus, and that's where the real adventure began. From Pooja claiming she wouldn't meet me in front of everyone because of shyness to her becoming the permanent co-pilot in the journey of my life, our story unfolded with unexpected twists and turns. Here's to the quirkiest first meeting, proving that life-changing encounters often happen when you least expect them. No regrets, just love and laughter!

Marriage Proposal

15th September 2023

I meticulously planned a trip to Goa, thinking I was the master of surprises. Taking her to the beach, I confidently announced we were heading to Thalassa for a magical evening. Little did she know, the real magic was about to unfold right there on the sandy shores. With a setup that screamed "romance goals" and a ring hidden like a treasure, I, the perpetually shy guy, found myself bending on my knees. Expressing love turned me into a bumbling mess of words, but hey, who needs eloquence when you've got a ring and a beach sunset? She said yes, probably because she found my awkwardness endearing or simply wanted to rescue me from my shaky-kneed proposal stance. Cheers to love, surprises, and the occasional stumble on the way to forever!
